# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers of Axiom pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, experience level, or identity.

We are committed to an open and professional environment focused on building useful, ethical intelligence tooling.

---

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Use clear, professional, and constructive communication
- Respect differing viewpoints and approaches
- Accept feedback gracefully and give it thoughtfully
- Focus discussions on the technical merits and the project's goals
- Report security issues privately and responsibly

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Contributing code or features designed for unauthorized surveillance or harm
- Misrepresenting the project or its capabilities
- Sustained disruptive behavior in issues, PRs, or discussions

---

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a private advisory or contacting the maintainers through the repository's [Security tab](https://github.com/osint-intell/Axiom/security).

Maintainers will review reports confidentially and may take action including comment removal, temporary ban, or permanent exclusion from the project.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other official communication channel related to Axiom.
